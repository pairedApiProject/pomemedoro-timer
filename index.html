Pomemedoro Technique

How to Use the Pomemedoro Technique
  1. Write a task in the box below that you would like to work on. You're allowed a maximum of five tasks in your list.
  2. Set the timer for how long you would like to work on the task (the default is 25 minutes). You can increase or decrease the time by clicking on the plus and minus buttons under Work Duration. Press RESET to update the timer.
  3. Press the START button.
  4. Work on the task continuously until the alarm rings.
  5. Take a 5 minute break. Congratulations, you've completed one pomodoro!
  6. When your break is over the timer will start counting down another work duration.
  7. After completing four pomodoros (steps 4 and 5), take a longer break (15 - 30 minutes), then start again from step 4.
  8. Once you've completed your task, check it off.
